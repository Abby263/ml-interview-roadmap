## Focus on what moves outcomes

A 30-day plan is not for mastery. It is for reducing obvious risk. You should bias toward the topics that show up most often and have the biggest downside when answered poorly.

That usually means:

- metrics and leakage
- SQL and coding patterns
- transformers, RAG, and evaluation basics
- one or two reusable system design structures

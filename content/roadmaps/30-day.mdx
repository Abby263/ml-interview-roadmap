## Focus on what moves outcomes

A 30-day plan is not for mastery. It is for reducing obvious risk. You should bias toward the topics that show up most often and have the biggest downside when answered poorly.

That usually means:

- Week 1: statistics, metrics, experimentation, traditional ML, leakage, SQL, and Python review
- Week 2: deep learning, transformers, and the MLOps basics needed to discuss production models
- Week 3: GenAI and LLMOps, including prompting, RAG, evaluation, guardrails, tracing, routing, cost, and safety
- Week 4: ML system design case studies, mocks, project storytelling, and weak-area repair

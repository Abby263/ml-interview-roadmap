## Where to compress

In a 60-day plan, you do not have time to study everything evenly. Keep the refresh blocks short and spend the bulk of the time on the categories most likely to fail the loop:

- coding speed
- system design structure
- GenAI evaluation and architecture

## Review loop

By the halfway mark, you should already be doing mini-mocks. Waiting until the final week makes the compression strategy much less effective.

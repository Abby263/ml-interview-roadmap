## Where to compress

In a 60-day plan, you do not have time to study everything evenly. Keep the refresh blocks short, but do not skip the sequence that interviews implicitly assume:

- Days 1-8: statistics, experimentation, optimization, and evaluation math
- Days 9-20: traditional ML, feature engineering, SQL, Python, and ML-from-scratch coding
- Days 21-28: deep learning and transformers
- Days 29-38: MLOps, data quality, training orchestration, model registry, serving, monitoring, and rollback
- Days 39-47: GenAI fundamentals, prompting, RAG, fine-tuning, agents, and guardrails
- Days 48-53: LLMOps, eval gates, tracing, routing, cost, safety, and privacy
- Days 54-60: ML system design cases, mocks, project storytelling, and behavioral prep

## Review loop

By the halfway mark, you should already be doing mini-mocks. Waiting until the final week makes the compression strategy much less effective.

## Product framing

A good answer starts by clarifying what the home feed is optimizing. Watch time, session starts, long-term retention, and creator diversity can all push the system in different directions.

## Multi-stage ranking

Recommendation interviews are usually strongest when you separate the system into:

1. candidate generation
2. ranking
3. re-ranking or policy layer

This gives you room to discuss latency and model complexity at different stages instead of pretending one model does everything.

## Monitoring

Mention both model metrics and product metrics:

- watch time
- completion rate
- repeated impressions
- creator diversity
- serving latency

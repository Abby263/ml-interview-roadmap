## Clarify the decision point

The first question is what the model influences. Does it block the payment, send the case to manual review, or just assign a risk score?

That single choice changes the cost of false positives and therefore the right operating threshold.

## Features and freshness

Fraud systems live or die on feature freshness. Mention recent card activity, merchant or device history, velocity features, and graph or account-linkage signals.

If the latency budget is tight, separate features into:

- synchronous online features
- cached or precomputed aggregates
- slower enrichment paths used for review or secondary models

## Monitoring

Production monitoring should include:

- system latency
- score distribution shifts
- approval and decline rates
- downstream confirmed fraud labels
- reviewer queue growth
